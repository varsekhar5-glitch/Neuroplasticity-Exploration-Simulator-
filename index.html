Neuroplasticity · a living science

The brain is not fixed.
It rewires itself.

For most of history we believed the adult brain was set in stone, its wiring permanent, its damage forever. It isn't. Neuroplasticity is the living proof that the brain reshapes itself through the choices we make every day: how we move, what we eat, how we think, how we heal.

Every projection inside is a teaching model, not a prediction. Coefficients are calibrated to real published research (cited in Science & Model Notes) but are not validated against real patients. Built to spark scientific curiosity, not to forecast your own biology.

Structure corrector

In ApoE4, Arg112 repositions Arg61 to form a salt bridge with Glu255, the "domain interaction" that makes ApoE4 pathogenic. A small-molecule corrector (e.g. PH002) breaks this bond so ApoE4 behaves like ApoE3. This is the removable pathogenic part your prompt asked to find.

Parkinson's, nigrostriatal dopamine

Substantia nigra dopaminergic neurons die → dopamine falls → tremor & rigidity. Aerobic exercise & meditation partly restore dopamine and slow decline (2nd paper: John Pepper).

Progressive nigral degeneration, the core PD lesion. ~60–80% lost before motor symptoms appear.
↑ dopamine, ↑ BDNF, delays motor progression.

Molecular AD, plaques & tangles

Extracellular amyloid-β fibrils clump into senile plaques; intracellular tau detaches from microtubules into neurofibrillary tangles, collapsing transport.

Higher = more Aβ fibrils, more tangles, destabilized microtubules.
Reduces amyloid load, tau, and oxidative stress.
